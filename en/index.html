Our approach

Three pillars, one method.

We are neither a school nor a tutoring service. We are a structured community that takes charge of its children's academic path with the same rigour as an elite program.

i. Pedagogy

Individualized plans, measurable indicators.

Targeted tutoring, themed camps, coding, preparation for science competitions. Each child progresses at their own pace, with a file that builds step by step.

ii. Events

Learn, celebrate, pass it on.

Five signature gatherings shape our year: Nuit des Défis, the Merit Walk, the Math Games, Summer Academy, Mentor for a Day.

iii. Community

Many families, one movement.

A solidarity of Afro-Canadian parents, mentors and volunteers. One child's success inspires all the others — we're building a movement, not a service.

Pedagogical pillar

Rigour that is taught, never endured.

We believe that rigour is liberating, as long as it is taught with patience and embodied by role models our children recognize themselves in.

Personalized plans, weekly small-group sessions, preparation for national and international math, science, logic and programming competitions, coding workshops, world languages: every program is built to reveal potential, not standardize it.

The BlackGenius Math Games

The BlackGenius Math Games are not a competition — they're a training program designed as a game. Our conviction: mathematics is best learned when experienced as a pleasure, not an ordeal.

Modeled on video-game logic — progressive levels, challenges, rewards, escalating difficulty — the program guides each child at their own pace, from basic mental math to advanced reasoning.

Consistency pays off. Every session played builds the child's file. The most diligent and high-performing children win prizes awarded publicly at the Nuit des Défis, and can access our scholarship fund reserved for our nonprofit's top achievers.
Who is it for?
Every child in our nonprofit, from elementary to high school.
How?
Online, ongoing, accessible anytime from the BlackGenius Canada platform.
