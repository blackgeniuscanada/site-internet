Support BlackGenius Canada.

Every donation brings us closer to a documented cohort of Afro-Quebecer children admitted to the world's best institutions. Your support funds scholarships, camps, trainers and our Math Games.

Our commitment

Total transparency.

Every dollar received is tracked and published in our annual report. 87% of funds go directly to children's programs; the rest funds coordination and compliance.

  • Official tax receipts once charitable status is obtained (targeting 2028, CRA).
  • Audited annual report published publicly.
  • One-time or recurring donation — you stay in control.
  • Option to earmark: Math Games, summer camps, scholarships, mentorship.

Your impact

Concretely, what does your donation fund?

  • $25Covers an annual educational kit (notebooks, geometry tools, digital access) for an elementary-school child.
  • $50Funds one hour of targeted one-on-one tutoring in math or science.
  • $100Lets a child take part in the Math Games: registration, transportation and materials.
  • $250Covers one week of Summer Academy camp for a scholarship child.
  • $1,000Funds a full annual membership scholarship for a lower-income family.
  • $5,000Makes you a sponsor of a Math Games edition: visibility, presence at the ceremony, mention in the annual report.
