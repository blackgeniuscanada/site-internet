Eleven families,
one movement.

Behind BlackGenius Canada: committed parents, volunteer coordinators, and soon a scientific committee. Here are the people who bring this collective to life every day.

Founding families · 2026

The eleven families who sparked the movement.

In 2026, eleven families of African descent pooled their resources, their time and their standards to give their children what they had been searching for in vain: a framework for academic excellence rooted in their identity.

i. Founding family

The K. family

Mother: HR consultant · Father: civil engineer. Two children, Grade 6 and Secondary 2. Coordinators for the elementary level.

ii. Founding family

The M. family

Mother: teacher · Father: entrepreneur. Three children in high school. Coordinators for science competitions.

iii. Founding family

The D. family

Mother: lawyer · Father: physician. One child in Secondary 4. Legal coordinators (non-profit incorporation).

iv. Founding family

The N. family

Mother: financial analyst · Father: architect. Two children in elementary school. Treasury coordinators.

v. Founding family

The B. family

Mother: pharmacist · Father: chemistry teacher. Three children. Summer Academy coordinators.

vi. Founding family

The S. family

Mother: family physician · Father: computer science researcher. Two children in high school. Coding and STEM coordinators.

The profiles above are generic descriptions out of respect for confidentiality. Photos and full names of founding families will be published after their individual written consent, expected at the October 2026 Merit Walk.
Operational coordination

The volunteer team running day-to-day life.

General coordination

Collective leadership

Running committees, following up with families, partner relations. Role held on an annual rotation among founding families.

Pedagogical coordination

Academic follow-up

Level diagnostics, individualized plans, liaison with tutors. Handled by the collective's parent-teachers.

Event coordination

Organizing the five signature gatherings

Nuit des Défis, the Merit Walk, the Math Games, Summer Academy, Mentor for a Day. A rotating logistics team.

Scientific committee · 2027

Guaranteeing academic rigour.

Starting in 2027, an independent scientific committee — made up of practicing teachers, doctoral candidates and science professionals — will oversee the design and grading of the BlackGenius Math Games tests.
